# Dylan Reference Manual Errata

Errata for mistakes of inclusion (typos, syntax errors, ...) are included directly
in the DRM.  Errata for mistakes of exclusion (lack of clarity etc.) are linked to
this document so that the DRM can remain largely in its originally published state.
Also, since we don't know the original DRM authors' intentions, the comments here
will often be based on the way specific Dylan implementations work, and the notes
here may therefore refer to those implementations.
